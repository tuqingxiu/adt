Super Seat
Future-oriented seats

Super seat
is a multifunctional high-end car seat independently developed
by Chongqing Yanfeng Adient.
Starting from the users’ needs and the application scenario,
we aim to meet customer’s different needs of entertainment and work,
so as to bring you a new experience of smart, convenient and comfortable seats.
Technologically sleek design
Smooth smart APP control
Convenient wireless phone charging
Five intelligent modules
Welcome module
When the door is opened, it will automatically rotate 45° for easy entry
Storage Modules
A number of ingenious storage designs provide greater support for your entertainment and mobile work.
Comfort module
With massage, lumbar support, leg rest, ventilation, flexible to adjust to provide ultimate comfort.
Multi-directional adjustment
Multi-directional adjustment and seat memory function, can realize 45° and 180° double-angle flexible rotation, and quickly adjust the comfortable position in the car.
Air Purification
The air purifier is installed on the backrest to monitor the real-time air inside the vehicle, automatically purify the air, so you can breathe fresh air in the car.
